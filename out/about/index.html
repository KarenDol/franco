About us

Frank Capasso|Founder|1940 - 2022

A Legacy of Restoration Expertise

Frank Capasso & Sons was founded in 1970 by Francesco Capasso, built on a commitment to skilled craftsmanship, accountability, and pride in the work. From the beginning, the company focused on masonry and concrete work where precision and experience matter most.

Over the decades, FCS evolved into a trusted restoration firm serving commercial, institutional, residential, and public clients throughout Connecticut and the surrounding region. The company's work has consistently centered on restoring, stabilizing, and extending the life of existing structures—often in occupied, aging, or sensitive environments.

Today, the company is led by Francesco Capasso's two sons, who continue to guide the firm with the same standards and values that shaped it from the start, while strengthening its capabilities for modern restoration challenges.

A Family Tradition Lives On

1960'S-1990'S GROWTH & TRANSITION

The company expands its expertise into larger and more complex restoration projects, earning trust across commercial and institutional markets.

2000'S TURNING POINT
