Built on a Proven Legacy of Masonry & Concrete Restoration for Over 50 Years

Family Owned and Operated Since 1970

Frank Capasso & Sons, Inc. is a Connecticut-based masonry and concrete restoration firm specializing in the repair, stabilization, and preservation of existing structures. For over five decades, we've been trusted by commercial, institutional and public clients where durability, safety, and long-term performance matter most executed by experienced crews who understand the complexity of working within occupied and aging structures.
